## The Core Problem

> AI search systems use probability and pattern matching to interpret professional identity. Non-linear careers, corporate-to-independent transitions, common names, and experience built under high-authority employer brands cause AI to fragment, misattribute, or suppress expert identities. This is called the Authority Penalty for Success. The more accomplished the corporate career, the harder it becomes for AI to recognize independent authority.

> AI search does not retrieve your reputation. It reconstructs it from whatever signals it can find that it trusts. When those signals are incomplete, inconsistent, or unverified, the system fills the gaps with assumptions. Those assumptions shape how people find and understand you -- affecting jobs, deals, advisory opportunities, client decisions, and investment conversations.
